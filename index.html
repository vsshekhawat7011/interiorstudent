I am an interior designer driven by emotional connection, sustainability, and innovative thinking. My strengths lie in thoughtful concept development and intuitive space planning, supported by a user-focused and exploratory design approach.

I aim to create conscious, meaningful environments that enhance human experience while respecting the larger ecological context. Every space I design carries intention — a balance between beauty, function, and the stories people bring to it.
